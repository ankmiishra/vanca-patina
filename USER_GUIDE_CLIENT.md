- All items you've added
- Quantity for each item
- Price per item
- Line total (quantity × price)
- **Cart Total** at the bottom

#### Managing Cart Items

**Change Quantity:**
- Use + button to increase quantity
- Use - button to decrease quantity
- Quantity cannot exceed available stock
- Quantity cannot go below 1

**Remove Item:**
- Click "Remove" or trash icon 🗑️
- Item disappears from cart
- Quantity updates

**Keep Shopping:**
- Click "Continue Shopping" button
- Go back to Shop page
- Browse and add more products

#### Cart Totals Explained
```
Subtotal:    ₹ 1,000         (sum of all items)
Tax (5%):    ₹    50         (calculated on subtotal)
Shipping:    ₹    75         (FREE if > ₹2,000)
─────────────────────────
TOTAL:       ₹ 1,125
```

**Shipping Policy:**
- FREE shipping on orders above ₹2,000
- ₹75 shipping on orders below ₹2,000
- Estimated delivery: 3-5 business days

### 4. CHECKOUT PROCESS

1. Ensure cart has items
2. Click "Proceed to Checkout" button
3. Login required - enter your credentials if not logged in

#### Entering Shipping Address

- **Address:** Your complete street address

## 🆘 TROUBLESHOOTING

### Login & Account Issues

**Problem: "Forgot my password"**
- Currently not implemented
- Contact support to reset password
- Or create new account with new email

**Problem: "Can't register - email already used"**
- Email already registered
- Try logging in with that email
- Or use different email address

**Problem: "Can't login - invalid credentials"**
- Check email spelling
- Check CAPS LOCK is off
- Ensure password is correct
- Verify email exists in system

### Cart Issues

**Problem: "Cart is empty"**
- Products not added
- Cart cleared after order
- Try adding product again

**Problem: "Can't add out of stock product"**
- Product is currently unavailable
- Cannot order 0 stock items
- Wait for restock or try different product

**Problem: "Cart total is wrong"**
- Check quantity × price calculations
- Tax calculated at 5% of subtotal
- Shipping: ₹75 if < ₹2,000, FREE if > ₹2,000

### Checkout Issues

**Problem: "Payment failed"**
- Check internet connection
- Verify payment method (card details, balance)
- Try different payment method (UPI, Net Banking)
- Contact your bank

**Problem: "Order not created"**
- Order should be created after payment
- Check your dashboard orders
- Check email for confirmation
- Contact support with Order ID if available

**Problem: "Can't access checkout - not a customer"**
- Must be logged in as regular user
- Cannot checkout as admin
- Login or register as customer

### Product Issues

**Problem: "Product image not showing"**
- Image failed to load
- Try refreshing page
- Check internet connection
- Contact support

**Problem: "Can't find product"**
- Try different search terms
- Check spelling
- Use filters (category, price)
- Product might be out of stock

### Order Issues

**Problem: "Order status not updating"**
- Admin hasn't updated it yet
- Wait for next update
- Check email for status notifications
- Contact support with Order ID

**Problem: "Order never arrived"**
- Check status (should be "Delivered")
- Check shipping address was correct
- Wait 5 business days
- Contact support with Order ID

### Payment Issues

**Problem: "Razorpay not opening"**
- JavaScript issue - refresh page
- Try different browser
- Try UPI/Net Banking instead
- Clear browser cache

**Problem: "Payment taken but order not created"**
- Payment processed successfully
- Order should be created automatically
- Check your orders immediately
- Contact support immediately if missing

**A:** Products can be returned within 7 days if unused and in original packaging. Contact support.
